Blog Content Guidelines

All blog content must:

  • Be based on personal experience and authentic wisdom
  • Align with GyG's philosophy of experience-based learning
  • Be respectful, encouraging, and constructive
  • Focus on personal development themes
  • Be original content written by you
  • Be free from promotional or commercial content
  • Undergo approval before publication
