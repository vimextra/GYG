GyG Syllabus: Experience-Based Learning

Our mentorship program is built on shared experiences rather than traditional materials. Each session focuses on practical wisdom and real-life application of core personal development principles.

Philosophy of Learning

GyG has no traditional material because its lessons are based on experiences. Our upcoming book "DIAMOND IN YOU" will complement these sessions, but the heart of our program lies in experiential learning and shared wisdom.

"Our Time on Earth is Limited. We don't know what tomorrow holds, lest we are called to be with the Almighty, let us leave an undeniable mark on the planet. So we need to learn and deliver now..."

Self-Awareness

Focus: Understanding your inner world

Core Concept: The ability to focus on yourself and how your actions, thoughts, or emotions do or don't align with your internal standards.

Learning Method: Personal reflection, group discussions, and experience sharing

Self-Development

Focus: Continuous personal growth

Core Concept: The process in which a person grows or changes and becomes more advanced through their own efforts.

Learning Method: Goal setting, skill building, and progress tracking through mentorship

Growth Mindset Skill Building
03

Self-Mastery

Focus: Developing inner discipline

Core Concept: The ability to control one's own desires or impulses: self-control, self-discipline.

Learning Method: Practical exercises, habit formation, and accountability partnerships

Discipline Self-Control

Law of Deliberate Creation

Learning Method: Visualization techniques, intention setting, and manifestation practices

Law of Abundance

Core Concept: There's an unlimited supply of resources, wealth, and opportunities available to everyone.
