Frequently Asked Questions

How do I become a life mentor?

Contact us through this form or call directly. We'll schedule an interview to understand your life experiences and wisdom, conduct a background check, and provide orientation on our experience-based mentoring approach.

What is experience-based learning?

GyG has no traditional materials - our lessons are based on shared experiences and wisdom. Mentors guide others through the five core areas using personal stories, life lessons, and practical insights.

How are donations used?

100% of donations go directly to program support: session resources, mentor support, program materials, and scholarships for those who need financial assistance to participate.

What are the five core areas?

The GyG syllabus covers Self-Awareness, Self-Development, Self-Mastery, Law of Deliberate Creation, and Law of Abundance. Each area focuses on different aspects of personal transformation.
