📋 Application Process

  • Application review and initial screening (5-7 business days)
  • Phone/video interview to discuss your life experiences and wisdom
  • Background check and reference verification
  • In-person meeting to understand your mentoring style
  • Mentor orientation on GyG philosophy and experience-based learning
  • Match with mentees and begin your transformational journey together

💡 What We Provide

  • Guidance on the GyG syllabus and experience-based mentoring approach
  • Access to "Diamond In You" book content when released
  • Ongoing support from our mentoring community
  • Platform to share your wisdom through our blog system
  • Recognition and appreciation for your contribution to transformation
